Soft Kenyan Chapati

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • Extra oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, and sugar in a bowl
  2. Add warm water gradually while kneading
  3. Knead for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic
  4. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes
  5. Divide dough into balls, roll into circles
  6. Brush with oil, fold, and roll again
  7. Heat pan and cook chapati on medium heat
  8. Flip until golden brown on both sides

Nutritional Information (per chapati)

Calories: 180 | Carbs: 30g | Protein: 4g | Fat: 5g
