Traditional Githeri

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled maize
  • 2 cups boiled beans
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot, saut√© onions until golden
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until soft
  3. Add curry powder and stir for 1 minute
  4. Add boiled maize and beans
  5. Mix well and add potatoes
  6. Add 1 cup water and simmer for 15 minutes
  7. Season with salt and serve hot

Nutritional Information (per serving)

Calories: 320 | Protein: 12g | Carbs: 60g | Fiber: 10g
